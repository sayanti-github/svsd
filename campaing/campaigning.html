Eligibility


JEXPO

Candidates seeking diploma degree admission in the Institute through JEXPO examination with valid rank are eligible to apply for admission for polytechnic.





Admission in Diploma first year under the Management Quota

Candidates seeking admission under the management quota should have required marks in secondary examination as per WBSCT & VE & SD (TED) norms.




Admission in Diploma second year (lateral admission)

Candidates having passed/ appeared in X + 2 board exam need to appear at ‘VOCLET’.
Passed 2 years’ ITI at a stretch after Madhyamik/ equivalent examination. There is no upper age limit for VOCLET Examination.
