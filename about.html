About Swami Vivekananda School of Diploma (SVSD):

Welcome to Swami Vivekananda School of Diploma, a renowned educational institution located in the serene surroundings of Malandighi, Durgapur. Spanning across 51 acres of lush greenery, our campus offers a picturesque setting for learning and growth.

This is an umbrella institution under Sanaka Educational Trust, established in 2012. At SVSD, we take immense pride in providing a diverse range of courses including Diploma in Civil Engineering, Diploma in Electrical Engineering and Diploma in Mechanical Engineering. All of these 3 years of courses are AICTE approved and affiliated to the West Bengal State Council of Technical and Vocational Education and Skill Development (Technical Education Division). With over 600 students on campus, SVSD has a distinct aim: to provide quality technical education and focus on grooming and training the students to meet the dynamic needs of society and industry through innovative approaches. The Institute focuses on disciplinary and multidisciplinary project-based learning to expand the boundaries of education with the support and guidance of its esteemed faculty members.

Our commitment lies in delivering quality education and fostering holistic development among our students. Our Placement Department is very active, when it comes to Placement for the institution. This ensures that our students have ample opportunities for internships, co-operative programs and job placements, giving them a head start in their careers. Companies like, Bajaj, Amazon, Yokohama, Kalyani, Jaya Hind, Genpact, SKH, Hadwise, Rakon, Mando, INDPAC India, Shyam Metalics, ICL CALIBRATION, Ronch, HiTechNext, Dhoot Transmissions, Anand, Universal Group, Jayaswal Neco Industries Ltd etc. are hiring students from SVSD. If you are looking for an institution that offers industry-relevant programs, emphasizes rigorous scientific pursuits, and encourages multidisciplinary thinking, SVSD is the place to be. Explore our website to discover more about our programs, facilities, and embark on an enriching journey of knowledge and excellence with us.



Vision of the Institution:

To be a leading Institute in the field of outcome based technical education by producing Diploma Engineers for societal needs and to encourage students for self-employment/start-ups.


Mission of the Institution:

  • M1: To educate and train students in Engineering and Technology domains.
  • M2: To develop employable Diploma Engineers as well as to prepare them for higher education by providing outcome based teaching-learning system.
  • M3: To encourage students to become successful entrepreneurs.
  • M4: To create a resource pool of professionals and responsible citizens.
